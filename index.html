Knockout mapping demo

This demonstrates that - at least in this trivial example - the two techniques for mapping a Knockout ViewModel are functionally equivalent. This uses a computed observable property added to the ViewModel to get the "firstName" (using String.split) and proves that in the ViewModel that exists and has appropriate functionality. It also shows that when un-mapping a ViewModel created in that way, we do not get the superfluous extra properties which were added by the mapping process; these are dropped from the VM when un-mapping.
